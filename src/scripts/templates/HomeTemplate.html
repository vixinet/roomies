As an erasmus student who doesn't speak Finnish (yet), it is not always easy to find a room and roomates in Finland. The information is out there on the internet, most part of the time in Finnish, incomplete or in platforms not adapted for the task of finding a roomate. Roomies.fi tries to solve this problem by giving to students a media to find easily rooms and roomates in Finland.

Find a Roomie

List your room

Add your room by filling our online form. This will allow your room to be visible on the website and have the maximum coverage.

Receive Roomie requests

Roomies will be able to see your room information, ask you questions, interact with you privately and send you "Roomies Requests"

Choose your Roomie

Choose through the requests the best Roomie for your flat. Don't worry about the other requests, we take care of warning them they were not picked.

Exchange contacts

We will comunicate you your new Roomie contact information and send your information to him. Your contact information stay private until you find your Roomie.

Find a Room

Browse the rooms

Browse the rooms available to find the perfect match for you. Use our filters to prioritize what is important for you! Budget, space, features, filter on what matters for you and find the perfect room.

Ask questions

Still some questions concerning the room or your future roomates? Simply ask a question and wait for an answer. All the questions are public so you can read through the ones already asked.

Send a Roomie Request

You found the perfect room? Send a Roomie Request to your (maybe) future roomate. Once he will choose his future roomate, you will be notified by email if you are the one.

Exchange contacts

We will comunicate you your new Roomie contact information and send your information to him. Your contact information stay private until you find your Roomie.
